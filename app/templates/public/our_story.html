The Beginning

Born from a Passion

Theo's Patisserie was founded on the belief that world-class pastry doesn't need to be flown in from Paris. Our pastry chefs trained in classical French technique and brought that knowledge home — to Noida, to Delhi, to India.

What started as a small patisserie in Sector 39, Noida, quickly grew into something bigger — a destination for those who believe that food can be both an art and an act of love.

Our Philosophy

Luxury in Every Bite

We believe luxury is not just a price point — it's a feeling. The weight of a perfectly tempered chocolate box in your hands. The first crack of a caramelised tart shell. The way a macaron gives way to reveal a perfectly flavoured ganache.

Every item in our display case is made in-house, by hand, every single day. No frozen doughs, no shortcuts — just technique, time, and an obsessive attention to quality.

India & France

Two Worlds, One Kitchen

What makes Theo's truly ours is the way we weave India into French forms. A Saffron Citrus Tart. A Kashmiri Walnut Opera Cake. A Mango Cardamom Macaron that tastes like summer in Noida.

We celebrate Indian flavours without apology — because the finest patisserie in the world should taste exactly like where it was made.
