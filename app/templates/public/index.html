Our Story

"Baking is not just what we do — it is who we are."

Born from a love of French patisserie and a deep appreciation for India's rich culinary heritage, Theos Patisserie brings together two worlds in every creation. Our pastry chefs train in classical technique, but their hearts belong to the spice markets of Noida.

Every macaron, every éclair, every hand-painted entremet that leaves our kitchen is a testament to this belief — that luxury is not just a price tag, but a feeling.
